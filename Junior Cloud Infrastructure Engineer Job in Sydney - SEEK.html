About us:
Our name comes from a combination of two words: Talent and Simplicity. Originally starting our operations in Melbourne back in 2018, Talentplicity now has offices in Melbourne and Sydney, catering to its clients across Australia, in every state and territory.

Talentplicity Consulting Services Delivery Team is growing rapidly. Due to several new projects starting we are looking to engage a junior/entry level Infrastructure Engineer be a pivotal part of these engagements. 

Special requirement
Due to the security requirements of our business partner, we can only consider applicants who are Australian citizens.

About the role
This is a fantastic and rare opportunity to join some of Australia’s top cloud engineers and grow you career in Cloud, Big Data, or DevOps. The successful applicant will join a customer facing team, guiding, and assisting several key clients with their newly created and well-established AWS environments.
To be successful with this requirement, you will display the following skills.
  • Degree and or relevant IT certifications.
  • A working knowledge of AWS cloud environment is crucial and or completed AWS foundation certification.
  • Some exposure to scripting (ideally with Python).
  • Excellent communication skills. 
  • 12-18 months experience supporting level 2/3 infrastructure would be greatly beneficial.
  • Ability to work on call with regular cycles in addition will receive on-call awards above annual salary.
How to apply
If you possess the above skills and experience, and we welcome you to join our team of consultants. For further information about this opportunity please feel free to email talent@talentplicity.com.au or call us on, from Melbourne (03) 9059 8038, from Sydney, any other locations in Australia and overseas on (02) 9121 6206.
  
For more jobs like this, please visit our website at www.talentplicity.com.au or at LinkedIn https://linkedin.com/company/Talentplicity.
